**Buying Guide:**
When choosing a mechanical keyboard, consider the following factors:

* Switch type: Romer-G, Kailh, or Cherry MX?

* Wrist rest: Do you prefer a built-in or detachable wrist rest?
* Media controls: Do you need dedicated media controls?
* Budget: How much are you willing to spend?

**FAQ:**

1. What is the best switch type for gaming?
Answer: Cherry MX switches are considered the gold standard for gaming, but Romer-G and Kailh switches are also popular options.
2. Do I need customizable lighting?
Answer: Customizable lighting can enhance your gaming experience, but it's not essential for typing or general use.
3. What is the difference between a built-in and detachable wrist rest?
Answer: A built-in wrist rest provides added comfort and support, while a detachable wrist rest offers flexibility and customization.
