Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best switch type for a mechanical keyboard?

The best switch type depends on personal preference. Romer-G Linear, Kailh Silver Speed, and Cherry MX Red are popular options.

What is the difference between a wired and wireless mechanical keyboard?

A wired keyboard offers a more stable connection, while a wireless keyboard provides greater flexibility and convenience.

What is the importance of polling rate in a mechanical keyboard?

A higher polling rate (e.g., 1000Hz) provides a more responsive and accurate typing experience.
