Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best switch type for gaming?

Cherry MX switches are considered the gold standard for gaming, but Romer-G and Kailh switches are also popular options.

Do I need customizable lighting?

Customizable lighting can enhance your gaming experience, but it's not essential for typing or general use.

What is the difference between a built-in and detachable wrist rest?

A built-in wrist rest provides added comfort and support, while a detachable wrist rest offers flexibility and customization.
